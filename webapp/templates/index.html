User Study on Automatic Music Accompaniment
- Information for Participants




About

The purpose of this study is to find out the quality of an automatic music accompaniemnt system, i.e. how well the automatic generated accompaniment fits a givin melody. This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of the model developed in our project "Automatic Music Accompaniment".

The study will takes you about 10 minutes. In the study, you will be asked to
- listen to several pieces of music audios and evaluate the quality of their accompaniments;
- give some comments on the automatic generated music, and our study;
- verify some weakness in the automatic accompaniment system;
- judge whether the automatic accompaniemnt system can help with musicians/non-musicians in their music creation.

We would like to invite you to be part of our research project. It is up to you to decide whether or not to take part. If you choose not to take part, there won't be any disadvantages for you and you will hear no more about it. If you decide to take part, please go to User Study and agree with our consent form first. You are still free to withdraw at any time without giving a reason.
