About

The purpose of this study is to find out the quality of an automatic music accompaniemnt system, i.e. how well the automatic generated accompaniment fits a given melody. This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of the model developed in our project "Automatic Music Accompaniment".

The study will take you about 10 minutes. In the study, you will be asked to
- listen to several pieces of music audios and evaluate the quality of their accompaniments;
- give some comments on the automatic generated music, and our study;
- verify some weakness in the automatic accompaniment system;
- judge whether the automatic accompaniemnt system can help with musicians/non-musicians in their music creation.

The user study page is tested in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari and Internet Explorer (lastest versions), If you have problem in playing audios, try chrome or another browser first.
