Queen Mary Ethics of Research Committee Ref: 1919

Thank you for considering taking part in our research. In the Information Sheet you can see an explanation on our project and user study. Please see it before you agree to take part.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at: lele.liu@se13.qmul.ac.uk

If you are willing to participate in this study, please read and agree to the following statements.

1. I agree that the research project named above has been explained to me to my satisfaction in verbal and/or written form.

2. I understand that if I decide at any other time during the research that I no longer wish to participate in this project, I can notify the researchers involved and be withdrawn from it immediately.

3. I have read both the notes written above and the Information Sheet about the project, and understand that the research study involves:
- Listening to several pieces of music;
- Completing a questionnaire asking me about my evaluation about the given music pieces.

4. I understand that the study will include the use of my name and year of musical training.


This information will be held and processed for the purpose of evaluating the performance of automatic music accompaniment system. I understand that any information I provide in confidential, and that no information that could lead to the identification of any individual will be collected during the project.
